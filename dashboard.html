About this app

This student task manager is designed to make it easier to juggle classes, assignments, quizzes, and projects in one place.

  • Manage Tasks: Create tasks with subjects, priorities, dates, and times.
  • Calendar View: See all of your deadlines on a calendar so you can plan ahead.
  • Smart Reminders: Get notified when deadlines are getting close (while the app is open).
  • Day / Night mode: Switch themes to match your study vibe.

Start by choosing one of the tiles above: manage today’s tasks, or check the calendar to see what’s coming up next.
